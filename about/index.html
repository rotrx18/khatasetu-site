Our Story & Mission

At KhataSetu, we are building the bridge between traditional bookkeeping and the digital future.

Who is KhataSetu?

Developed by ROTRX IT Solution, KhataSetu was born out of a simple observation: small business owners spend too much time struggling with paper-based registers (Bahi Khata). These physical books are easily lost, prone to calculation errors, and impossible to access remotely.

KhataSetu is a high-performance digital ledger application designed to give shopkeepers, freelancers, and entrepreneurs full control over their financial transactions with the speed and security of the cloud.

Our Mission

To empower 1 million small business owners globally by providing free, world-class financial tracking tools that improve cash flow and reduce accounting stress.

Why We are Different

While there are many "khata" apps available, KhataSetu focuses on three core pillars:

  • Global Accessibility: Unlike region-locked apps, KhataSetu is built for business owners worldwide.
  • Reliability: Our "Offline-First" technology ensures you can record a sale even in areas with no internet.
  • Privacy: We believe your business data belongs only to you. We use end-to-end encryption to keep your records private.
