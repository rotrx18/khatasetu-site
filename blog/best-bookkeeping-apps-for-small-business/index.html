What to Look for in a Bookkeeping App

1. Simplicity over Complexity

If an app requires a training course, it is probably too slow for a busy shop. Look for "One-Tap" entry systems where you can record a transaction in under 5 seconds.

2. Automated Reminders

The best apps don't just record debt; they help collect it. Built-in WhatsApp integration that nudges customers automatically when their payments are due is a must-have in 2026.

3. Real-Time Sync

Your data should be accessible on multiple phones simultaneously. This allows your shop staff and your partner to stay updated on the same ledger at the same time.
