What to Look for in a Bookkeeping App

1. Simplicity over Complexity

If an app requires a 2-hour training course, it’s not for you. Look for "One-Tap" entry systems where you can record a sale or an expense in under 5 seconds.

2. Automated Reminders

The best apps don't just record debt; they help collect it. Look for built-in WhatsApp or SMS integration that nudges customers automatically when their payments are due.

3. Multi-Device Sync

Your data should be accessible on your phone, your tablet, and your partner's phone simultaneously. This "real-time sync" is a standard requirement for businesses in 2026.
