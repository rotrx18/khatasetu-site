1. The Solo Business Reality in 2026

The freelance economy has exploded. Whether you are a graphic designer, a software consultant, or a content creator, you are running a business. One of the biggest mistakes freelancers make is mixing personal and business finances. A digital ledger acts as the boundary line, ensuring you know exactly how much your "business" is earning versus how much you are "spending."

3. When Freelancers Give Credit (Udhaar)

In many industries, you work first and get paid later. This is essentially extending credit to your clients. If you don't track this "Udhaar," you risk working for free. Digital ledgers allow you to set "payment terms" and track the aging of your invoices, ensuring that no client takes advantage of your time.
