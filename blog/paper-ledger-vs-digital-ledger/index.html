1. History of the Bahi Khata

2. Paper Ledgers: Benefits & Risks

While paper has no learning curve, it comes with hidden costs that can hurt a small business's bottom line.

Paper Pros:
  • No battery needed
  • Instant writing
  • Zero tech skills
Paper Cons:
  • Easily lost/burned
  • Math errors are common
  • No remote access
