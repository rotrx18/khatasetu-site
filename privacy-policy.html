1. Information We Collect

To provide an efficient digital bookkeeping experience, we collect the following data points:

a) User-Provided Information

  • Ledger Data: Transaction entries, credit/debit records, and business notes entered manually.
  • Contact Access: If granted permission, we access your contacts to help you easily link transactions to specific customers.

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Specs: Device type, Operating System version, and unique identifiers.
  • Performance Logs: Crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics to ensure app stability.
  • Usage Stats: Anonymized interaction data via Firebase Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process your data to ensure the app functions smoothly and securely:

  • Core Services: Maintaining and syncing your digital khata book.
  • Security: Monitoring for fraudulent activity or unauthorized access.
  • Monetization: Serving contextual advertisements via Google AdMob in the free version.
  • Analytics: Identifying features that need improvement.

3. Legal Basis (GDPR & CCPA)

We process information under the following legal frameworks:

  • Consent: When you provide permission for contact access or notifications.
  • Contract: To fulfill our service agreement to track your business records.
  • Compliance: To meet legal and tax reporting obligations where applicable.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal or financial data to third parties. We only share data with essential service providers:

  • Google Firebase: Crash analytics.
  • Google AdMob: For advertising purposes (AdMob Privacy Policy applies).
  • App Stores: Processing through Google Play or Apple App Store.

5. Data Security & International Transfers

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted cloud servers. Because we serve a global audience, your information may be processed on servers located outside your home country. We utilize industry-standard encryption to protect data during transit and at rest.

Retention: We keep your data as long as your account is active or as required by law.

6. Data Deletion & Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time. If you choose to delete your account, all associated ledger data will be permanently purged from our servers.
